How not to make 3 main mistakes when communicating with a woman on a Dating site

Nowadays, people are increasingly looking for relationships on various Dating sites. So many men overcome the fear of Dating in person, bypassing this stage. For some reason, many women in most cases ask the same question the question: "the Purpose of your acquaintance?" and based on the answer of the man, they form a dialogue further. Today we will talk about what absolutely cannot be said in response to this question:

Just chat

Time is the most valuable resource on the planet. For example, when I hear such words from a woman, then for some reason inside there is some irritation and I remember the video of Maxim Martsinkevich (Cleaver) on this topic. There he explains in detail his position on this phrase. So the woman does not want to spend extra time on communication, even though they love this business.

It is better to tell the truth that you are looking for a life partner with whom you are ready to build its further future. And in no case should you lie. Because all the secrets will come true sooner or later.

Let's meet, and then we'll see

After this phrase, the following thoughts appear in the woman's head: "I think that the product is some kind of product, that would look at me and decide to buy or not." Dating already assumes that you will evaluate all the advantages and disadvantages of each other. Therefore, this phrase is too erroneous.

Instead of this phrase, you can refer to her interests, and say that they have attracted you, so you want to meet and get to know each other better. This will show that you do not need a woman for one night, but that you are really interested in her.

When I saw your photos, I knew I only wanted you

I do not know if this tackle works or not, but there are not a few similar ones. When my friends showed me messages like this like, I just laughed at it. Even if, according to their friends, their type wrote to them, this phrase still immediately merged it. Now no woman will believe when they meet that she is the only one with whom a man communicates.

It is better to point out her cool style of clothing or unusual and memorable appearance. Can ask about her Hobbies, etc. People like to talk about themselves.
