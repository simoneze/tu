Skills that impress women

In our time, you will not surprise a girl with the presence of a car or a cool style of clothing. Girls are looking for something special, because they want to live with a smart, responsible and interesting man, not a guy from the cover. Of course, we are all different, and some like one type of man, and some like another. However, there is a certain set of skills that will impress almost any girl. And if you have several at once, then you will enjoy incredible success with women.

Today we will talk about these skills:

Foreign language proficiency

The skill is quite complex, because we can't learn it a foreign language in one day, but in a couple of months you can learn to speak it. The main thing is to be really interested and make a lot of effort. I remember on vacation with a girl, I started talking to the locals in English. She had a look on her face like I was a God to her. Although there is nothing wrong with that special.

Playing musical instruments

Very cool skill: be able to play musical instruments. For large companies, guys who own a guitar attract the entire female sex to themselves. Remembering the University, then near my headman, who played a great guitar, sat all the girls of the band. And he he was very popular with them. And at school, girls were always hanging around a guy who played the piano well. Even if you don't know how to play anything, you can always learn. Try to enroll in courses and you will see the result over time.

Ability to dance

Of course, not everyone can to dance, someone is afraid to look stupid, someone dances only after drinking alcohol, and after that there is nothing to be afraid of. But if you want to impress girls, you need to learn to dance. Please note that in cafes or clubs, guys who can dance well, collect all the attention of the dance floor. It's summer now and a lot of people take dance lessons and practice outdoors. And sometimes they even dance in public places, in a large group, to remove fears.

The ability to cook

This skill will be useful not only in your relationship, but also in your life as a whole. Moreover, the best cooks are men. & be sure to study all the cookbooks. It is enough to be able to prepare a delicious and easy Breakfast, a girl in the morning in bed. This gesture will pleasantly surprise her and add pluses to your piggy Bank. And if you know how to cook unusual dishes, then you will impress her 100%.

A Jack of all trades

If you live with a girl, you will be able to you will face household chores: screw in a light bulb, fix a faucet or nail a shelf, etc.And if instead of calling the master, you do everything yourself, then you will show the girl that you are a real man and can do everything yourself. She will be proud of you. Even if you didn't know how to do something before, then in our age of the Internet, you can view the training materials and do everything yourself.

Good physical shape

Girls like it when a man looks after himself and does sports. This gives him confidence, and girls a sense of security. You don't have to go to the gym 5 times a week to look good. Many it is enough to visit the gym 2-3 times a week or practice football, Boxing, etc.
