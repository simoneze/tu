How to distinguish female flirting from basic politeness

When you notice that a woman starts flirting with you, it is the first signal to act in her direction. But men sometimes confuse flirting with ordinary politeness.

It doesn't seem like a big deal, but there are broken hearts behind this mess many men who did not feel this difference. For example, a girl smiles at you or helps you pick up things that have fallen out of your hands, and you like a real male, ask her on a date and naturally get rejected. And you don't understand why this happened? You seem to have done everything right. But the bottom line is that men and women, they view interest in each other differently.

Of course, there are women for whom flirting is a common form of communication. You first think that she is playing this game, just with you, but then it turns out that you are the fifth person today who was rejected, thinking the same way. Such a game a woman can play on purpose, getting from it certain emotions and raising your self-esteem.

Today we will talk about how not to confuse flirting with ordinary politeness:

Eye contact

Long-term eye contact on the part of a girl, it is like a challenge that you would get to know her. Of course, this does not mean that you should stand with stopwatch and count how many seconds it looks in your direction. You will feel this for yourself when she often pays attention to you. Some also note accompanying gestures from the girl: she winds her hair around her finger, constantly touches it, bites her lip, etc. d.

Physical contact

Another point when a girl wants to draw attention to herself is the installation of physical contact from her. When she seems to casually take your hand, or for any reason, tries to touch you, then it's a good idea a clear sign of flirting. What you can't say when it just passes you in the store, accidentally hitting you.

Compliment

Compliments are always nice for all people. And of course, it seems more natural to us when we make them for girls, rather than Vice versa. When you notice that a woman has suddenly and unexpectedly become often to give you compliments, then this is the first call to interest on her part. But phrases like: what kind of cool or cool guy you are, you will definitely find a girlfriend does not count. Such phrases only imply that you are assigned to the friend zone.

Attention on you

When a girl wants to pay your attention if you are looking at yourself, then all her thoughts and actions will be directed only in your direction. That is, if there are a lot of people in the room, and she always tries to be near you, then this is 100% the girl's interest, and the mood for future relations with you.
